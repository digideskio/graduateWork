What Are Adjuvants?

Adjuvants are “components capable of enhancing and/or shaping antigen-specific immune responses” (Reed, 2013). I.e., additives to vaccines that can:

  • Decrease minimum effective dose
  • Enable more rapid immune response
  • Broaden host antibody responses
  • Increase magnitude of antibody response
  • Increase duration of vaccine protection
